New Initiative to Integrate Refugees into the Labour Market

In an ongoing effort to support refugee integration, our organization has launched a new initiative that aims to connect refugees with employment opportunities. Through partnerships with local businesses, we are creating a capacity building program that will prepare refugees for the job market by providing training in specific skills and ongoing support.

This initiative not only offers new hope for refugees, but also benefits the community by filling skills gaps in key sectors. We hope that this programme will serve as a model for other regions facing similar challenges
